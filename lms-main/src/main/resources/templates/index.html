Youth-led action against plastic waste

Mismanaged plastic waste is becoming a major threat to the well-being of our environment. Rapid urbanization and population make collection and waste management more challenging. Helping societies with the right knowledge and tools to better cope with their waste, is a key way to create a more sustainable future.

At “Changemakers – Youth-led action against plastic waste”, we empower students with the knowledge and tools to manage plastic waste sustainably. We want to engage youths to influence, inform and empower future generations.

In 2022, this platform has been launched as pilot with selected high schools in the metropolitan Kolkata region in West Bengal, India.
